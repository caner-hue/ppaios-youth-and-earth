## What this skill does
Long-form LinkedIn drafts (800–1200 chars) for Caner's profile or PP page. Operator voice, proof-led, no hype.

## Context
- Tone-of-voice + brand brain.
- Recent LinkedIn performance.

## Inputs
- Theme (e.g. "why most DTC offers underconvert").
- Source (a recent client win, framework, course transcript chunk).
- Target post date.

## Workflow
1. Lead sentence = the strongest claim. No throat-clearing.
2. Story or framework body (5–8 short paragraphs).
3. Specific numbers, real brand names, documented outcomes.
4. Closing thought, no fake question CTA.
5. 2–3 hashtags max, niche not generic.
6. `brand-voice-check`.
7. Save to Notion Content Calendar.

## Rules
- No "thoughts?" or "agree?" closers.
- No engagement bait.
- No emojis unless integral to the point.
- Caner's voice = operator, not consultant. "We did X. It worked. Here's why."
